The Visionaries

B J Sai Krishna & Sreelakshmi S S

Welcome to Kaliyanaa Kompany—where our partnership celebrates yours. Founded right out of our college campus by real-life partners Sai Krishna and Sreelakshmi S S, Kaliyanaa Kompany was built on a simple promise: to capture every single frame in memories. We aren't just another corporate wedding photography agency; we are a dedicated duo that understands the deep chemistry, emotion, and trust it takes to build a lifetime together.

As a team, we divide our creative roles perfectly to serve you better. While B J Sai Krishna leads our technical camera operations and lighting composition as Lead Photographer, Sreelakshmi handles our visual curation, aesthetics, and modern mobile content direction as Creative Director. This allows us to offer couples a completely integrated, stress-free photography and cinematic video experience on their big day.

From traditional ceremonies and backwater pre-wedding shoots to high-energy modern receptions across Kerala, we focus heavily on unscripted, natural, and raw candid moments. Let us document your love story, frame by frame.
